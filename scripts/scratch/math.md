Kelvin wave speed:
\begin{align}
    \frac{C_1}{C_0} = \frac{C_0 + \delta C}{C_0} &= 1.1\\
    \implies \frac{C_1^2}{C_0^2} &= 1.21
\end{align}

Let $D = C^{-2}$. Then:
\begin{align}
    \frac{\delta D}{D_0} &= \frac{C^{-2}_1-C^{-2}_0}{C^{-2}_0}\\
    &= \frac{C_1^{-2}}{C_0^{-2}}-1\\
    &= (1/1.21)-1\\
    &\approx -0.17
\end{align}
